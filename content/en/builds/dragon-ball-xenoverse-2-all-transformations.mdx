<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 All Transformations: Universal Awoken Skills">

In the sprawling universe of Conton City, mastering **dragon ball xenoverse 2 all transformations** is the ultimate path to power for your custom Time Patroller. Universal Awoken Skills can be equipped by any of the five playable races, providing massive strategic flexibility depending on whether your build focuses on Strike, Ki Blast, or Basic Attack damage.

**Kaioken** is the earliest universal form players can unlock. It scales across three distinct stages based on your current Ki pool. While it grants exceptional mobility and cheaper vanishing maneuvers, the continuous stamina drain makes it highly situational. Experienced players typically use it for short, high-pressure melee rushes rather than sustained combat.

**Potential Unleashed** remains the gold standard for balanced, low-risk gameplay. Requiring five bars of Ki to activate, it grants a flat 15% boost to all damage types and increases your Ki generation from basic combos. Because it features no passive resource drain or defensive penalties, it is widely considered the safest choice for high-level PvP and difficult Parallel Quests.

**Beast** represents the high-risk, high-reward glass cannon archetype. Introduced in a free update, this form grants an unparalleled 30% damage boost to all offensive categories. However, it also increases the damage you take by 20% and features a long, highly punishable transformation animation. Success with Beast requires creating safe openings or relying on team protection during multiplayer raids.

**Ultra Instinct** shifts the focus from raw offensive scaling to defensive utility. When active, your character automatically dodges incoming basic attacks at the cost of stamina. It also alters your basic charged Ki blasts and adds a unique counter mechanism. Since it does not provide direct damage multipliers, it is best utilized by defensive specialists.

**The Power to Overcome** is the latest endgame addition from the Future Saga Chapter 4 DLC. This form offers passive health regeneration when your health drops below 50%, alongside an unblockable sword strike that replaces your standard grab. Its signature Unleashed Mode grants a 12-second window of extreme Ki recovery and massive damage scaling, balanced by a temporary health drain penalty.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Saiyan Awoken Skills and Stat Scaling">

Saiyans possess the deepest roster of exclusive transformations, reflecting their central role in the Dragon Ball mythos. These forms are highly specialized, shifting your combat effectiveness toward physical strike pressure, ranged energy blasts, or basic combo loops.

**Super Saiyan (1-3)** scales based on your activated Ki level. Super Saiyan 3 offers a massive 20% boost to physical Strike attacks and Basic Attacks, alongside a 15% physical damage reduction. However, it severely cripples your passive Ki recovery, forcing you to rely on basic strikes or active charge skills to maintain your energy pool.

**Super Vegeta (1-2)** is the mirror image of the standard Super Saiyan line, optimizing your character for Ki Blast damage. Super Vegeta 2 provides a 17.5% boost to all Ki Blast Skills, making it the premier choice for beam-heavy builds. It features a less severe Ki recovery penalty than Super Saiyan 3, allowing for more consistent ranged pressure.

**Future Super Saiyan** acts as a highly efficient PvP option. It only features a single stage with modest 10% Strike and 5% Ki Blast buffs, but it increases your stamina recovery speed by 15%. This allows you to recover from defensive vanishes much faster than your opponent.

**Super Saiyan God** focuses entirely on basic physical combat. It grants an incredible 40% boost to Basic Attacks and a massive 50% increase to Ki recovery. It also introduces a timing-based combo mechanic where perfectly timed button presses deal explosive bonus damage. It does not scale your active skills, making it a highly specialized tool.

**Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan (Blue)** and its **Evolved** variant represent the absolute peak of Saiyan offensive power. Blue Evolved grants a staggering 35% boost to all damage types and a 40% increase to stamina recovery. To balance this overwhelming power, both forms continuously drain your Ki, requiring specialized Super Souls or active charge setups to prevent detransformation.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Race-Exclusive Awoken Skills">

Each of the non-Saiyan races features a highly unique, lore-accurate transformation tied to their specific faction headquarters in Conton City. These forms completely alter your character's moveset, defensive options, or projectile behavior.

**Turn Golden** is exclusive to the Frieza Race. It transforms your basic charged Ki blasts into highly lethal, high-velocity Death Beams. It also raises your Ki Blast Skill damage by 30% and significantly boosts your movement speed. This makes the Frieza Race exceptionally powerful in multiplayer Expert Missions and Raids, where keeping your distance is paramount.

**Purification** turns your Majin character into a replica of Kid Buu, completely replacing your custom skill set and combos with Kid Buu's aggressive, fast-paced moveset. While active, your stamina continues to recover even while performing attacks, allowing for relentless offensive pressure. However, you take 25% more damage, and a stamina break will immediately force you out of the form.

**Become Giant** allows Namekians to grow to massive proportions, dominating the battlefield. While giant, your health pool is replaced by your stamina bar, and you gain complete super armor against normal attacks. The form drains your stamina continuously at a 40% rate, meaning you must finish your opponent quickly before your stamina breaks.

**Power Pole Pro** is the Earthling-exclusive form. It summons the Flying Nimbus and equips the Power Pole, granting exceptional basic attack reach and high mobility. It features moderate damage-based super armor, but its overall damage output is relatively low compared to other racial forms, making it more of a utility option.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Every Transformation">

Unlocking the diverse array of Awoken Skills in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 requires a mix of story progression, faction quests, and mentor friendship farming. Follow this step-by-step route to claim the most powerful forms for your Time Patroller.

  <Step num="1" title="Attain Potential Unleashed">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Beast Form">
    Complete the main story campaign. Next, maximize your friendship levels with mentors Piccolo and Gohan & Videl. Speak to Piccolo at the Time Nest, select the extra spar option, and defeat Cell Max.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Ultra Instinct">
    Ensure your game is updated to the latest version. Locate Jiren (Full Power) in the Conton City plaza. Accept his special challenge battle and defeat him to claim the Ultra Instinct Awoken Skill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach Godhood (SSG, SSGSS, Evolved)">
    For Super Saiyan God, collect all seven Dragon Balls and wish to become a God. Maximize friendship with Goku, Vegeta, Gohan & Videl, Gotenks, and Pan, then speak to Beerus. For Blue and Blue Evolved, reach Level 90 and 95 respectively, max your friendship with Whis and Vegeta, and speak to Whis.
  </Step>
