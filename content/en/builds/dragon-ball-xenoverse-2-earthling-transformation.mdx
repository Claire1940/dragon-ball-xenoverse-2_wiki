<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Power Pole Pro Quest Guide">

To begin the quests for the Power Pole Pro, you must progress through the main story until Conton City's Time Rifts are fully unlocked. Once open, fly over to Hercule's House and locate Great Saiyaman 1, Great Saiyaman 2, and Jaco the Galactic Patrolman hanging out in the courtyard.

You will need to complete a series of four training missions. These missions involve defending the city alongside the Great Saiyaman squad, fighting off various enemies, and eventually proving your strength to the heroes themselves.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 35 and Progress Story">
    Advance the main story until you unlock flight in Conton City. Ensure your Earthling character is at least level 35 to trigger the final stages of the questline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete the Initial Training Trio">
    Speak with Great Saiyaman 1 and complete the first three training missions. These involve fighting the heroes, defeating the Ginyu Force, and beating three custom patrolmen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Final Quest">
    Once you reach the level threshold and complete the prior fights, an exclamation mark will appear over Great Saiyaman 1. Accept the quest titled "The Power to Overcome".
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Power Pole Pro Mid-Battle">
    During the final battle against Gohan, Videl, and Jaco, you will be prompted to activate the Power Pole Pro skill. Finish the fight using the transformation to unlock it permanently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The progression is straightforward but requires you to complete each step in order. Below is the detailed breakdown of the quests you must complete at Hercule's House to earn your awoken skill.
