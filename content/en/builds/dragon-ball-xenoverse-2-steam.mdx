<Section icon={Target} title="Downgrading Version Steps">

Follow these steps to revert your game version to a stable state. This process requires using the Windows 'Run' command to interact with the Steam console directly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open Steam Console">
    Press Win+R on your keyboard, type `steam://open/console`, and hit Enter to launch the hidden Steam console tab.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Manifest">
    Visit the SteamDB website to find the specific patch number or manifest ID that corresponds to your preferred game version.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Download">
    In the console, enter `download_depot 454650 454651 [ManifestID]` to begin downloading the specific game files.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Replace Files">
    Navigate to your SteamApps/content folder to locate the downloaded files and overwrite your current game installation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Backup Required">
Always create a backup of your current `bin` and `data` folders before overwriting files to avoid losing progress or custom data.
</Callout>
