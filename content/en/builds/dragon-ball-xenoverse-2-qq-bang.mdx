<Section icon={Shield} title="The Save Data Backup Strategy">

Because Super Mix Capsule Z and high-tier clothing are expensive or time-consuming to farm, you should never "blind mix" without a safety net. This process, often called save-scumming, allows you to reclaim your materials if the RNG gods are not in your favor.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Upload Save Data">
    Before entering the Mixing Shop, upload your current save to the Cloud (PlayStation/Xbox) or copy it to a USB/separate folder (PC).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mass Mix">
    Enter the shop and use all your materials. Create 10-20 QQ Bangs in one go.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Evaluate Results">
    Check your inventory. If you didn't get a +22 or higher QQ Bang that fits your build, **do not leave the menu** if possible, or simply close the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Download Save">
    Download your previous save from the Cloud. You will have all your capsules and clothing back, ready for another attempt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
You can hold up to 250 QQ Bangs. If you hit the limit, the game will force an auto-save when you exit the menu. Always sell your "trash" QQ Bangs to the item shop to keep your inventory clear and prevent forced saves.
</Callout>
