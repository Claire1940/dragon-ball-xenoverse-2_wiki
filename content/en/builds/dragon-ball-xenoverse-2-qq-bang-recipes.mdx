<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Save Scumming Guide">

Because 6-star crafting is heavily reliant on RNG, you can easily waste dozens of rare clothing items and Super Mix Capsule Zs without getting the stats you want. To bypass this, you must utilize the save data backup method. 

This technique allows you to preserve your hard-earned materials and repeat the crafting process indefinitely until you roll a perfect QQ Bang.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Back Up Your Save Data">
    Before entering the Mixing Shop, close the game. Upload your save data to the PlayStation Cloud (PS Plus required) or copy it to a USB drive (PS4 version). This secures your current inventory of materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Mixing Shop and Craft">
    Boot up the game, head directly to the Mixing Shop, and craft your chosen recipe in large batches. Do not leave the crafting menu while doing this, as exiting the menu triggers the game's auto-save feature.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Analyze Your Results">
    Inspect the crafted QQ Bangs in your inventory. If you successfully rolled a +24 stat combination that fits your character build, you are finished. You can now exit the menu and let the game auto-save.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Restore Save Data on Bad Rolls">
    If you did not get the desired stats, immediately close the game from your console dashboard *before* exiting the mixing menu. Download your backed-up save data from the Cloud or USB to restore all of your spent materials, then repeat the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Auto-Save Trigger">
The game enforces a strict inventory limit of approximately 250 QQ Bangs. If your inventory hits this cap while mass-producing items, the game will automatically kick you out of the mixing menu and force an auto-save, trapping your spent materials. Keep your inventory clear before starting a crafting session!
