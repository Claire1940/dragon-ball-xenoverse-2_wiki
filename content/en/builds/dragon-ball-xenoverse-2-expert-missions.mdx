After completing the initial tutorials, new Expert Missions will begin appearing across Conton City. Each mission manifests as a floating purple orb crackling with dark energy. <Section icon={Shield} title="Expert Mission Progression & Core Mechanics">

Understanding the visual cues of Expert Mission orbs is essential for tracking your completion status. An incomplete mission orb is surrounded by violent black lightning. Once you have successfully cleared the mission under the correct hosting conditions, the orb transforms into a bright, stable purple light. 

These missions feature distinct super boss mechanics designed to challenge even the most experienced Time Patrollers. Unlike standard Parallel Quests, you cannot rely on healing capsules or battle items during these encounters, forcing a heavy reliance on specialized character builds and team coordination.

| Orb Appearance | Completion Status | Progression Granted | Playstyle Recommendation |

After you successfully clear the first nine Expert Missions, the Supreme Kai of Time will consolidate your progress. All previously beaten missions will be merged into a single giant purple orb located directly behind the Time Machine Station. This hub makes replaying your favorite missions for rewards significantly more convenient.

<Callout type="info" title="Boss Health Scaling">
The game scales the boss's total health pool based on your lobby type. When playing offline, the boss has significantly less health to compensate for the lower efficiency of AI teammates, making solo progression highly viable.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="TP Medal & Demon Realm Crystal Farming Route">

TP Medals are the premium progression currency in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2, used to purchase top-tier skills, custom partner options, and figures in the Hero Colosseum. Expert Missions 16 and 17 are universally recognized as the fastest farming routes in the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Farming Super Soul">
    Equip the Super Soul "40 ton weights!" to double your score and experience points, or use "I've got something extra special for you!" to increase rare drop rates for Demon Realm Crystals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Expert Mission 17">
    Select "In the Realm of the Gods: Vegeta". Vegeta behaves less aggressively than Goku in Mission 16, making this run safer and faster to complete.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spam Emperor's Death Beam">
    At the start of the match, charge your Ki using Maximum Charge or Ultimate Charge. Transform immediately, then continuously spam Emperor's Death Beam to lock Vegeta in place.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deflect Giant Ki Blasts">
    If Vegeta launches a Gigantic Ki Blast, immediately use Soul Punisher or dual Destructo-Discs to push the blast back instantly, ending the phase.
  </Step>
</Steps>
