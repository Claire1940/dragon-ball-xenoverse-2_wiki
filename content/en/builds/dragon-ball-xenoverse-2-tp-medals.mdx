<Section icon={Target} title="Fastest Farming Routes: Expert Missions">

If you want to maximize your hourly intake of **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 TP Medals**, you must unlock and master Expert Missions. Specifically, Expert Mission 16 (In the Realm of the Gods: Vegeta) and Expert Mission 17 (In the Realm of the Gods: Goku) are the gold standards for efficiency. These missions are straightforward boss fights that do not involve the complex "gimmicks" found in earlier or later Expert Missions, such as the mind-control or the giant Ki blast struggles that can significantly slow down your clear time.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Expert Missions">
    Progress through the main story until you unlock the ability to fly in Conton City. Complete the first 15 Expert Missions to unlock the high-tier "Realm of the Gods" challenges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Optimal Skills">
    For solo farming, equip high-damage Ultimate Attacks. "Emperor's Death Beam" or "Super Spirit Bomb" are highly recommended for their ability to pin down the boss and deal massive damage quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Your Boss">
    Select Mission 16 (Vegeta) if you prefer a boss that stays relatively stationary. Select Mission 17 (Goku) if you have a build that excels at chasing down mobile targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Loop">
    Enter the mission, spam your high-damage Ultimates, and clear it within 2 minutes. You will receive 16 TP Medals upon completion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

While both missions reward the same amount, many players prefer Mission 16 because Vegeta tends to use his "Brainwash" skill less frequently than Goku, leading to more consistent sub-2-minute runs. If you are playing during a "2x TP Medal Event," these missions become incredibly lucrative, yielding 32 medals per run.

| Mission Number | Boss | Base Reward | Estimated Time | Medals Per Hour |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **EM 16** | Vegeta | 16 Medals | 1:45 - 2:15 | 320 - 480 |
| **EM 17** | Goku | 16 Medals | 2:00 - 2:30 | 300 - 450 |
| **EM 18** | Broly | 18 Medals | 3:30 - 5:00 | 210 - 270 |

</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Spend TP Medals Efficiently">

Once you have accumulated a healthy stack of **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 TP Medals**, the next step is knowing where to invest them. The game offers several "sinks" for this currency, ranging from cosmetic upgrades to essential power boosts. Prioritizing your spending will ensure you don't run out of medals when a rare skill appears in the rotating TP Medal Shop.

1.  **Leveling with Tosok**: Located behind the Mixing Shop, Tosok can level up your character instantly in exchange for Zeni (up to level 80) and TP Medals (level 81 and above). This is the most common use for medals in the late game.
2.  **The TP Medal Shop**: This shop features a rotating inventory of skills, clothing, and Super Souls that cannot be found anywhere else. Some of the best skills in the game, such as "Divine Lasso" or "Meditation," are exclusive to this shop.
3.  **Partner Customization**: You can spend medals to unlock new skills, stat spreads, and color options for your Mentors, allowing you to create powerful custom partners for Parallel Quests.
4.  **Hero Colosseum**: If you enjoy the figure-based mini-game, TP Medals are the primary currency for summoning new figures and upgrading their abilities.

| Spending Priority | Category | Cost Range | Impact on Power |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High** | Leveling (Tosok) | 20 - 200 per level | ★★★★★ |
| **High** | Essential Skills | 150 - 500 per skill | ★★★★☆ |
| **Medium** | Partner Stats | 10 - 50 per unlock | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Low** | Cosmetics/Clothing | 10 - 100 per piece | ★☆☆☆☆ |

<Callout type="info" title="Tosok's Pricing">
The cost of leveling up with Tosok increases as you reach higher levels. For example, jumping from level 98 to 99 costs significantly more than 81 to 82. Always keep a reserve of at least 1,000 medals if you plan on maxing out a new character quickly.
</Callout>
