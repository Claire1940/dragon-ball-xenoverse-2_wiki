Beyond these three pillars, you can further boost your score by performing specific actions during the fight. These "Evaluation Criteria" are cumulative, so stacking as many as possible is the best strategy for players who might struggle with the time limit.

| Evaluation Criteria | Requirement | Point Value |
|---|---|---|
| Ultimate Finish | Finish last enemy with Ultimate | High |
| Transformed Finish | Finish last enemy while transformed | Medium |
| Accurate | Maintain 80% accuracy with attacks | Medium |

<Section icon={Target} title="Super Class Advancement Test Walkthrough">

The Super Class test is the final hurdle in the advancement series. Unlike previous tests, simply winning is not enough to unlock the most prestigious rewards. This test pits you against a gauntlet of powerful foes, including Pan, Jaco, Broly, and Beerus.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement with Pan">
    Pan is the first opponent. Use this phase to build your Ki and Stamina. Since she has lower health, avoid using Ultimates here unless you have a massive Ki reserve. Focus on building a high combo count to trigger the "Combo Adept" bonus early.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Managing Jaco and Broly">
    Jaco is elusive but fragile, while Broly is a tank with high super armor. The key here is to use an Ultimate Skill to finish off at least one of these two. This ensures you meet the "Multiple Ultimate Finishes" hidden requirement for the Z-rank in this specific mission.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Final Showdown with Beerus">
    Beerus is the final boss and has high evasion. Save at least three bars of Ki for a guaranteed Ultimate hit. Wait for him to exhaust his stamina or use a heavy stamina break combo before launching your finisher.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To secure the Z-rank here, expert players recommend finishing at least three of the four enemies with an Ultimate Skill. While the general rule for other tests is only the final enemy, the Super Class test has a much higher point threshold.

| Opponent | Strategy | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Pan | Build Ki / High Combos | Low |
| Jaco | Quick Ultimate Finish | Medium |
| Broly | Stamina Break then Ultimate | High |
| Beerus | Final Ultimate Finish | Critical |

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
Using a transformation like Super Saiyan 3 or Golden Form while delivering the final blow adds the "Transformed Finish" bonus, which can be the deciding factor between an S and a Z rank.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Rewards: Potential Unleashed and Beyond">

Passing the **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 advancement test** at the Super Class level rewards you with one of the most versatile skills in the game: Potential Unleashed. Unlike racial transformations, this Awoken skill can be used by any character class and provides a balanced buff without the drawbacks of high Ki drain or reduced defense.

| Attribute | Potential Unleashed | Super Saiyan Blue |
|---|---|---|
| Damage Buff | +15% All Damage | +25% All Damage |
| Ki Recovery | Increased | Significantly Reduced |
| Defense | No Change | Slight Reduction |
| Stamina Regen | Increased | No Change |
| Activation | 5 Bars of Ki | 5 Bars of Ki |

Additionally, completing this test is a mandatory requirement for unlocking Broly as an instructor. Broly can be found floating near the Mushroom Desert area once the test is completed. He provides some of the most powerful physical strike skills in the game, making him essential for melee-focused builds.

<Callout type="info" title="Skill Analysis">
