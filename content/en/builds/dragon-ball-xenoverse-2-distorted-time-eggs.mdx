Acquiring all five **dragon ball xenoverse 2 distorted time eggs** requires a methodical approach. Because the progression systems for each rift run parallel to one another, you can work on multiple eggs simultaneously. 

Follow this structured roadmap to efficiently complete the faction requirements and claim your rewards.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Satan House - Great Saiyaman Missions">
    Travel to Hercule's House and speak with the Great Saiyaman (Gohan in disguise) and Great Saiyaman 2 (Videl). Agree to join their heroic squad. You must complete all of their patrol missions, which involve fighting various villains and protecting the citizens of Conton City. Once their questline is fully resolved, speak to Hercule (Mr. Satan) on the patio. He will hand over the first Distorted Time Egg, explaining that a masked man dropped it on his property.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Capsule Corporation - Vegeta's Training">
    Head to the Capsule Corporation rift and locate Vegeta in the training area. He will challenge you to sparring matches to test your strength. These battles unlock gradually as your character reaches specific level milestones (typically levels 30, 40, 50, and 60). After defeating Vegeta in his final Super Saiyan Blue sparring match, speak to Bulma nearby. She will give you the second egg, reasoning that it is safer in your hands than near her battle-hungry husband.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Guru's House - Namekian Defense">
    Visit Grand Elder Guru's House and speak with Nail at the entrance. Your objective is to defend the Namekian Dragon Balls from waves of invaders. You must complete these defense missions multiple times. After defending the house successfully through several cycles, Nail will step aside and grant you entry to Guru's chamber. Grand Elder Guru will draw out your hidden potential (increasing your attribute cap) and eventually bestow the third egg upon you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Majin Buu's House - Building a Family">
    Go to Majin Buu's House and speak with him. He desires to start a family but needs food to do so. You must collect food items scattered around Conton City (such as fish, apples, and carrots) or purchase them from the Item Shop. Feed Buu until his food gauge fills up, causing him to produce a child. You must repeat this process until Buu has at least five children. Once his family is sufficiently large, Buu will happily hand over the fourth egg.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Frieza's Spaceship - Frieza Force Ascendancy">
    Enter Frieza's Spaceship and speak with Appule or Lort to gain entry. You must join the Frieza Force and work your way up the ranks. This involves choosing a faction (such as Zarbon or Dodoria), defeating your rivals, and eventually serving Frieza directly. Complete all subsequent missions, including Frieza's training forms and his ultimate transformation requests. Once Frieza's rule is absolute and his questline ends, speak to the soldier Navel to receive the fifth and final egg.
  </Step>
</Steps>
