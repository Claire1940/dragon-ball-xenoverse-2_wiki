<Section icon={Zap} title="Universal Awoken Skills: The Big Three">

In the 2026 meta, three universal skills stand above the rest. These can be equipped by any race, providing flexibility for Earthlings, Namekians, Majins, and Frieza Race characters who want to match the power of Saiyans.

| Skill | Ki Cost | Attack Modifier | Defense Modifier | Unique Mechanic |

| **The Power to Overcome** | 500 | +30% (Stage 2) | +20% All | Unblockable Grab / Infinite Ki Phase |

### The Power to Overcome (New DLC)
This skill, introduced in the Future Saga Chapter 4, is unique because it functions in two distinct phases. Initially, it provides a solid 20% defensive buff and a 5% speed increase. However, re-inputting the command triggers the "Unleashed" state. Your Ki bar turns blue, providing infinite resources for a short duration, but your health begins to slowly deplete.

### Ultra Instinct
Ultra Instinct remains the king of defense. While it provides no raw damage boost, the ability to auto-dodge basic attacks (at the cost of 100 stamina) is invaluable in PvP. It also changes your basic Ki blasts to Soaring Fist, which is excellent for keeping pressure on opponents from a distance.

### Beast
If you are looking for raw destruction, Beast is your go-to. It offers a massive 30% boost to both Strike and Ki Blast damage. The trade-off is that you become a "glass cannon," taking 20% more damage from all sources.

<Callout type="warning" title="Health Warning">
When using the second stage of **The Power to Overcome**, keep a close eye on your health bar. The health drain can be lethal if you don't finish the fight quickly or have a Super Soul that provides health regeneration.
</Callout>

### Frieza Race: Turn Golden
This remains the premier choice for Ki Blast builds. It transforms your basic Ki blasts into high-speed beams that can interrupt most enemy actions. It is widely considered the best race-specific skill for clearing Parallel Quests quickly.

### Namekian: Become Giant
This is a niche but powerful transformation for territorial control. While giant, you are immune to flinching from basic attacks, but your stamina acts as your health bar. If it hits zero, you revert to normal and enter a guard-break state.

<Callout type="info" title="Majin Tip">

<Section icon={Target} title="Unlock Guide: How to Obtain Every Skill">

Unlocking all **dragon ball xenoverse 2 awoken skills** requires a mix of story progression, friendship levels with instructors, and specific DLC purchases.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level Milestones">
    Most basic transformations (SSJ, Giant, Golden) require reaching Level 40 and completing the specific "Time Rift" quests (e.g., Capsule Corp for Saiyans, Guru's House for Namekians).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maximize Friendships">
    To unlock skills like Potential Unleashed, you must pass all Advancement Tests at the Orange Star High School with a Z-Rank. For SSG, you must maximize friendship with Goku, Vegeta, Gohan & Videl, Gotenks, and Pan.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Special Requirements">
    - **Beast**: Max friendship with Piccolo and Gohan/Videl, then complete the special battle at the school.
    - **Ultra Instinct**: Defeat Jiren in the special battle located in the Conton City market area (Level 99 recommended).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="DLC Content">
    **The Power to Overcome** is exclusively locked behind the **Future Saga Chapter 4** paid DLC. You must complete the story missions associated with Ultra Super Villain Goku to unlock it for your CaC.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Fast Leveling">
Use the NPC **Tosok** in Conton City to level up your character quickly using Zeni (up to level 80) and TP Medals (up to level 199). This is the fastest way to meet the level requirements for endgame skills.
</Callout>

</Section>
