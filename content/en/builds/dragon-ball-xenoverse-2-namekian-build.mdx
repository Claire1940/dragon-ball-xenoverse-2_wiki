Reaching level 180 provides a massive pool of attribute points, allowing you to reach the 200-point cap in specific categories. For a Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 Namekian build, the goal is to create a "Behemoth" that simply refuses to die. By dumping 200 points into Health, you maximize the efficiency of health-regen Super Souls.

| Attribute | Points | Reasoning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Health** | **200** | Essential for Namekian racial traits and tanking Ultimates |
| **Ki** | **0** | Rely on QQ Bangs and meditation skills for Ki bars |
| **Stamina** | **114** | Provides an optimal number of stamina bars for evasives |

| **Strike Supers** | **0** | Minimal investment needed if focusing on Ki Blasts |

The 114 points in Stamina is a calculated choice. In the 2026 meta, having exactly enough stamina for 7-8 bars is often better than having 10, as it allows for faster stamina breaks recovery while still providing enough resources for multiple Vanishes.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Equipment and QQ Bang Crafting">

To truly optimize a Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 Namekian build, you cannot rely on standard clothing stats. You must craft a Level 6 QQ Bang. The goal is to negate the -5 penalties found on high-end armor while stacking +5 bonuses on the stats that matter most to Namekians.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Materials">
    Collect two pieces of **Battle Suit Bardock** or one Bardock piece and one **Lightart Suit** piece. These have the highest probability of yielding Health and Stamina bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Use Super Mix Capsule Z">
    You must use a **Super Mix Capsule Z** to guarantee a Level 6 result. These can be farmed from Tour Missions or Parallel Quest 83.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Verify the Results">
    Aim for a result that provides **+5 Health**, **+5 Stamina**, and **+5 Basic Attack**. Any penalties in Strike or Ki Blast are acceptable if the core three are maxed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
