<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced PC Optimization and Keybinds">

While the default **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 controls PC** layout is functional, many competitive players choose to customize their settings to better suit the keyboard and mouse environment. The most common complaint involves the default mapping of the "Lock-on" and "Descend" keys, which can feel clunky during fast-paced 3v3 battles.

| Feature | Recommended PC Setting | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lock-on** | Mouse Button 4 | Faster target switching in crowds |
| **Descend** | C or V | Easier to reach than Left Alt |
| **Mouse Sensitivity** | 30-45% | Prevents "jittery" camera during flight |
| **V-Sync** | Off | Minimal input lag for perfect blocks |
| **Framerate Cap** | 60 or Variable | Smoother animation for timing hits |
