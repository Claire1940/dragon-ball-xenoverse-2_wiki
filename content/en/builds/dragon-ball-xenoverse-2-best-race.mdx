<Section icon={Shield} title="Tactical Alternatives: Namekians and the Frieza Race">

While Saiyans take the spotlight, the **Namekian** and **Frieza Race** options offer distinct gameplay loops that can be more effective in specific scenarios. Namekians are the defensive powerhouses of the game, featuring higher health pools and the ability to regenerate health when it drops below a certain threshold. Their stretchy limbs provide a reach advantage that can catch opponents off guard during standard combo strings.

The Frieza Race, on the other hand, is built for speed. They possess the fastest movement speed in the game, which increases further as their health decreases. Their unique "Turn Golden" transformation is arguably one of the best single-stage transformations, as it fundamentally changes their basic Ki blasts into high-speed Death Beams.

<Section icon={Target} title="The Specialized Tier: Majins and Earthlings">

The Majin and Earthling races occupy a unique niche in the 2026 meta. Majins are defined by their high defense and stamina management. Male Majins have more health but take more damage when their stamina is low, making them a "high-risk, high-reward" choice. Female Majins, conversely, have the fastest stamina recovery in the game, making them incredibly difficult to pin down in competitive play.

Earthlings are often criticized for feeling like "copy-pasted" Saiyans, but they possess a very powerful passive: automatic Ki regeneration. This allows them to use Supers more frequently without needing to manually charge as often. Their unique transformation, Powerpole Nimbus, is often considered the weakest in the game, but their base combo strings are among the most reliable for landing Ultimates.

| Race Variant | Best Stat Focus | Unique Trait |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Female Majin** | Strike / Stamina | Fastest stamina break recovery |
| **Male Majin** | Health / Defense | High damage resistance at full stamina |
| **Earthling** | Balanced / Ki | Passive Ki regeneration over time |

<Callout type="warning" title="Build Caution">
Avoid the **Purification** transformation for Majins unless you are comfortable with your entire move set being replaced by Kid Buu's skills. It removes your customizability during combat.
</Callout>
