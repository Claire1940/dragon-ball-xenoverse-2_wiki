<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Unlock All Awoken Skills">

Awoken Skills are the defining mechanic of combat, transforming your character and granting massive stat multipliers. While some transformations are universal, others are strictly locked to specific races. Unlocking these forms requires completing faction quests within the five Time Rifts scattered around Conton City.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 40 and Complete Faction Quests">
    Progress through the main story until you reach Level 40. This unlocks the specific questlines inside the Time Rifts. For Saiyans, visit Vegeta at Capsule Corporation; for Namekians, visit Guru's House; for Frieza Race, complete missions on Frieza's Spaceship.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Super Class Advancement Test">
    To unlock Potential Unleashed, the premier universal transformation, you must earn a Z-Rank on all five standard Advancement Tests at the Patroller Academy. Once completed, clear the newly unlocked Super Class Advancement Test.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maximize Instructor Friendship for Beast and Ultra Instinct">
    To unlock the Beast form, maximize your friendship levels with Piccolo and Gohan & Videl, then complete the special spar at the Time Nest. For Ultra Instinct, clear the special Jiren challenge located in Conton City.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Choosing the right transformation depends on your build's primary damage type and your ability to manage resources. While Beast offers the highest raw damage multiplier, it also increases the damage you receive by 20%, making it a high-risk option in competitive PvP.
