<Section icon={Zap} title="The Math of Ki and Stamina Bars">

One of the most common mistakes when allocating **dragon ball xenoverse 2 attribute points** is "wasting" points that don't result in a full bar of energy. Since most skills require exactly 100 Ki (1 bar), having 5.5 bars is functionally identical to having 5 bars in most scenarios.

**Ki/Stamina Point Thresholds (Level 80-99):**
- **42 Points**: +1 Bar
- **84 Points**: +2 Bars
- **125 Points**: +3 Bars

**Ki/Stamina Point Thresholds (Level 120+):**
At level 120, characters naturally start with 6 bars of Ki. The scaling changes slightly to accommodate the higher level cap.

| Desired Bars | Points Required (Lv 80) | Points Required (Lv 120) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **7 Bars** | 84 Points | 45 Points |
| **8 Bars** | 125 Points | 90 Points |
| **10 Bars** | N/A (Requires QQ Bang) | 180 Points |

<Callout type="info" title="Stamina Management">
Be careful with high Stamina. While it allows for more Vanishes, a "Stamina Break" will last significantly longer because the regeneration rate is static. 6 to 8 bars is generally considered the "sweet spot" for competitive play.
</Callout>

</Section>
