<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Super Saiyan Questline">

Once you have completed the Frieza Arc and reached Level 30, the path to unlocking your true potential begins. This process requires navigating the Capsule Corporation rift and proving your combat prowess to Vegeta through multiple sparring sessions.

| Quest Name | Level Recommended | Main Opponent | Key Unlock/Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Vegeta's Sparring** | Level 30 | Vegeta (Base) | First trial completed |
| **Vegeta's Secret** | Level 42 | Vegeta (Super Saiyan) | Family dialogue unlocked |
| **Saiyan Awakening** | Level 42 | Goku & Vegeta | Super Saiyan Awoken Skill |

Follow these precise steps to progress through the questline and secure the transformation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Training at Capsule Corporation">
    Travel to the Capsule Corporation platform floating in Conton City. You can reach this area by flying directly to it or by speaking with a Transfer Shop Clerk robot to teleport instantly. Once inside the rift, locate Vegeta standing near the gravity chamber. If you are at least Level 30, a green event icon will appear over his head. Speak to him to begin your first sparring match. You must defeat him in his base form to prove your worthiness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Level Up to Trigger the Second Match">
    After completing the first fight, Vegeta will refuse to train you further until you grow stronger. You must level up your character to progress. While some players report the next event appearing slightly earlier, reaching Level 42 is the most reliable threshold to trigger the next phase. Keep an eye on the top-right corner of your screen while exploring Conton City; an "Event at Capsule Corporation" notification will appear when the next step is ready.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Consult with Vegeta's Family">
    Return to Capsule Corporation and fight Vegeta a second time. In this battle, he will transform into a Super Saiyan, significantly increasing his speed and damage. After defeating him, speak with him again. He will express frustration that you have not yet unlocked the form and direct you to speak with his family. Walk a short distance to find Bulma near the clothing shop, then locate Kid Trunks running nearby. Talk to both of them to gather insights on how Saiyans tap into their rage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Saiyan Awakening Mission">
    With the insights gained from Bulma and Trunks, return to Vegeta. He will challenge you to a final, decisive battle alongside him against Goku and himself. During this three-on-two encounter, your character will automatically trigger the Super Saiyan transformation mid-fight. Harness this newfound power to defeat both legendary warriors. Once the battle ends, you will permanently unlock the Super Saiyan Awoken Skill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Super Saiyan Mechanics & Ki Thresholds">

The standard **dragon ball xenoverse 2 super saiyan** Awoken Skill is unique because it features three distinct tiers within a single equipped skill slot. The specific tier you transform into is determined entirely by the number of Ki bars you have charged when activating the skill. 

| Form | Ki Required | Strike Damage | Ki Blast Damage | Ki Recovery Rate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Super Saiyan 1** | 3 Bars | +10% | +5% | Reduced |
| **Super Saiyan 2** | 4 Bars | +15% | +7.5% | Significantly Reduced |
| **Super Saiyan 3** | 5 Bars | +20% | +10% | Drastically Reduced |

Understanding these thresholds allows you to manage your resources effectively during fast-paced battles.
