<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 Blue Evolution Overview">

The **Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan (Evolved)** form, commonly referred to as **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 Blue Evolution**, represents the pinnacle of Saiyan power for Create-a-Characters (CaC). Introduced as an advanced version of the standard Super Saiyan Blue, this Awoken Skill allows players to push beyond their limits, mimicking Vegeta's powerful transformation from the Tournament of Power.

Visually, the form is striking. Your character's hair shifts to a deeper, richer royal blue compared to the standard SSGSS form. Additionally, the eyes gain visible pupils and turn a darker shade of blue, while the aura becomes more intense, featuring distinct sparkles and a glowing blue outline. Beyond the aesthetics, the raw statistical advantages make it a favorite for players focusing on high-damage output in both PvE and PvP scenarios.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Unlock Blue Evolution in 2026">

Unlocking the **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 Blue Evolution** form is a multi-step process that requires significant progression through the game's mentor system. Unlike basic transformations that are handed out during the story, this Awoken Skill demands high-level character stats and maxed-out relationships with key Dragon Ball characters.

The primary mentor involved is Whis, but your history with Vegeta is equally important. Even though you may have unlocked the standard Blue form, you must return to the training grounds to prove you have surpassed the standard divine limits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 95">
    Your CaC must be at least Level 95. If you are stuck at Level 80, 85, or 90, visit Tosok in Conton City and pay TP Medals to instantly level up, or complete Guru's House missions to expand your level cap.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maximize Mentor Friendships">
    You must reach the maximum friendship level with both **Vegeta** and **Whis**. This is best achieved by bringing them as AI partners on Parallel Quests or repeatedly completing their final training lessons while wearing their specific clothing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Obtain Standard SSGSS">
    You cannot skip directly to Evolved. You must already have the standard Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan transformation unlocked from Whis (requires Level 90 and max friendship with Whis).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Speak to Whis">
    Once all conditions are met, locate Whis at the forest near the school. He will acknowledge your growth and grant you the Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan (Evolved) Awoken Skill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Race Restriction">
Whis will give this skill to any character race (Earthling, Namekian, etc.) once the requirements are met, but **only Saiyans** can actually equip and use the transformation in battle.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Managing the Ki Drain Mechanic">

The defining characteristic of **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 Blue Evolution** is its aggressive Ki drain. While the form grants unparalleled power, it constantly consumes your Ki reserves, eventually forcing a reversion to base form if your Ki hits zero. To use this form effectively, you must build your character around "Ki Auto-Recovery" mechanics.

The game calculates Ki recovery as a negative modifier in this form. In its current 2026 state, the drain is approximately 18% slower than it was at launch, but it still requires active management.

| Strategy Type | Recommended Skill/Item | Effect on Blue Evolution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Skill Support** | **Meditation** | Massively increases Ki recovery for a set duration. |
| **Skill Support** | **Divinity Unleashed** | Increases Ki gain from landing hits/taking damage. |
| **Super Soul** | **"Time for a little nap!"** | Provides constant Ki Auto-Recovery (Sleep status). |
| **Super Soul** | **"Right, then... Let's begin..."** | Heavy Ki recovery when health is above 75%. |
| **Super Soul** | **"Our two strengths..."** | Permanent Medium Ki recovery with no downsides. |

<Callout type="success" title="Optimized Recovery">
Using the Super Soul **"Our two strengths aren't just added together"** (from the Legendary DLC pack) almost entirely nullifies the drain of Blue Evolution, allowing you to stay transformed indefinitely without needing to stop and charge.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Blue Evolution vs. Other Transformations">

With the addition of forms like **Beast** and **Ultra Instinct**, many players wonder if **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 Blue Evolution** is still the top choice for Saiyans. While Beast offers a higher damage ceiling (around 40%), it also significantly increases the damage you take. Blue Evolution remains the "Goldilocks" form—providing elite damage with a defensive safety net.

| Form | Damage | Defense | Drain/Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **SSGSS (Evolved)** | +35% | +10% Resistance | High Ki Drain |
| **Beast** | +40% | -20% Resistance | 500 Ki (One-time) |
| **Potential Unleashed** | +15% | +5% Resistance | None |
| **Ultra Instinct** | +0% (Utility) | +0% | Stamina Drain on Dodge |
| **Super Saiyan God** | +0% (Basic Atk +60%)| +0% | None |

Blue Evolution is particularly effective in **Raid Quests** where you have ample time to charge Ki or receive Ki from allies. In these environments, the 35% damage boost to Ultimates like *Final Kamehameha* or *Prominence Flash* is unmatched by almost any other Saiyan-specific Awoken Skill.

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Tip">
If you are running a Strike-based build, combine Blue Evolution with **Justice Combination**. The 35% boost applies to every hit of the rush, leading to massive health bar depletion in seconds.
</Callout>
