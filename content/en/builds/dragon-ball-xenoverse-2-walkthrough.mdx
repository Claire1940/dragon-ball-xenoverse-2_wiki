<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Fundamentals and Early Game Route">

The combat loop in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 revolves around **Stamina Management**. In 2026, players who master the "Stamina Break" dominate both PvE and PvP. A light stamina break is essential for landing Ultimates, while a heavy stamina break provides the longest window for high-damage combos.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Elder Kai's Tutorials">
    Visit the Patroller Academy immediately. These tutorials teach you Just Guards, Vanishing, and Stamina Breaks which are vital for surviving early Time Patrols.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Saiyan Saga">
    Focus on the main story until you defeat Raditz and Nappa. This unlocks the first batch of Instructors and the Skill Shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Parallel Quests (PQ)">
    Alternate between Story Chapters and PQs. PQs provide the Zeni and XP needed to stay ahead of the level curve.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Pass Advancement Tests">
    Return to the Academy whenever a new test is available. Reaching the "Super Class" rank is required to unlock the best Awoken Skills.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Warning">
Never empty your stamina gauge casually. If your stamina is broken, you lose all defensive options. Use **Instant Rise** or **Mach Dash** as low-cost evasives to reset neutral without wasting bars.
</Callout>
