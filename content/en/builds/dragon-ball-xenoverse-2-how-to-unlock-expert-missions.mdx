The most common reason players get stuck is the "Lobby Rule." Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 handles progression strictly: you only unlock the next mission if you are the "Owner" of the session. If you simply use the "Quick Match" feature or join a friend's invite, you will receive rewards like TP Medals and items, but the game will not flag the mission as completed for your progression path.

To ensure you unlock the next mission, you have two primary methods:

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Unlock Process">

Unlocking all missions requires a linear approach. You cannot skip Mission 5 to do Mission 10. Each successful completion reveals the next orb location on your map. If you lose track of where the next orb is, check the map for the pink/purple "Rift" icon.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear the Tutorials">
    Complete the first two missions provided by the Supreme Kai of Time near the Patroller Academy. These teach you how to handle "Mind Control" and "Large Ki Blasts."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hunt the Great Apes">
    The early Expert Missions (like Mission 3: Assault of the Great Apes) are found near the Mushroom District. Clearing these is mandatory to move toward the mid-game missions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Host Status">
    After every win, check your map. A new orb will appear in a different sector of Conton City (e.g., Bamboo Forest, Ajisa District, or the Sky).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Final Bosses">
    Missions 16 and 17 feature Vegeta and Goku in their Blue forms. These are the final standard missions and offer the highest TP Medal rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>
