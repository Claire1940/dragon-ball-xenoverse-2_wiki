Parallel Quest 79, titled "Great Ape Festival," is the most efficient quest for farming Zeni. This mission tasks you with defeating three Great Apes, followed by an optional battle against three Time Patrollers who drop the coveted Hercule Badges.

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Great Apes">
