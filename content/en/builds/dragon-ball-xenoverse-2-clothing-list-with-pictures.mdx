  <Step num="1" title="Parallel Quest Farming">
    Most character outfits drop as random rewards from Parallel Quests (PQs). Check the "Rewards" tab before starting a mission. Ensure you get a **Z Rank** and trigger the **Ultimate Finish** to maximize your drop chances.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The TP Medal Shop">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Majin Buu's Family">
    Feeding the yellow Majin children at Buu's House is the only way to get sets like **Supreme Kai's Clothes**, **Dabura's Clothes**, and **Uub's Outfit**. Different food types influence the rarity of the drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Online Raids and Events">

  </Step>
</Steps>
