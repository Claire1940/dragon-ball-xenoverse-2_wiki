<Section icon={Swords} title="Beast Transformation Properties and Stat Boosts">

The **dragon ball xenoverse 2 beast transformation** is not merely a cosmetic change; it completely alters your character's combat style, combo potential, and stat distribution. When activated, the form changes the user's hair (for Human and Saiyan male/female avatars), turns their eyes crimson red, and surrounds them with a brilliant magenta and rosé aura punctuated by cyan and red lightning particles. Your character also adopts a unique, aggressive combat stance.

Unlike standard transformations, activating the Beast form consumes exactly 500 Ki (5 bars) upon startup, rather than just requiring you to possess that amount. This is a crucial distinction that players must manage during intense battles.

| Attribute | Modifier | Combat Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Ki Cost** | Consumes 500 Ki | Requires 5 full bars to activate and drains them instantly |
| **Attack Boost** | Massive Increase | Significantly escalates both Strike and Ki Blast damage |
| **Ki Recovery** | Highly Boosted | Increases the amount of Ki generated from landing basic attacks |
| **Defense Debuff** | Increased Damage Taken | Reduces overall defense, making you take roughly 20% more damage |

In addition to raw stat changes, the Beast form completely overwrites your default combo strings. The most notable addition is a highly cinematic, high-damage special combo that can be executed by inputting Heavy, Light, Heavy, Heavy, Heavy, Heavy, Heavy (or Triangle, Square, Triangle, Triangle, Triangle, Triangle, Triangle on controllers).

| Combo Type | Default Damage | Beast Form Damage | Combat Utility |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Basic Square String** | 3,648 | 4,700 | Excellent for basic ground and air pressure |
| **Basic Triangle String** | 3,600 | 4,700 | Fast knockback setup for long-range ultimates |
| **Special Beast Combo** | N/A | 9,300 | High-damage sequence ending with a devastating kick |

<Callout type="info" title="Teleportation Mechanics">
Similar to the Super Saiyan family of Awoken Skills, the Beast transformation grants automatic teleportation to your standing Light attacks and fully charged Heavy attacks. However, unlike Super Saiyan, charging up your Super or Ultimate attacks will not trigger a teleport.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="PvP Strategies and Super Soul Synergies">

While the offensive capabilities of the **dragon ball xenoverse 2 beast transformation** are undeniable, utilizing it in high-level PvP or difficult PvE Parallel Quests requires careful tactical planning. The primary drawback of the form is its exceptionally long transformation animation. During this activation window, you are entirely vulnerable to Charged Heavy Attacks and Reverse Burst Dashes, which can result in an instant guard break.

To mitigate these risks and offset the 20% defense penalty, players should structure their builds around specific Super Souls and racial advantages.
