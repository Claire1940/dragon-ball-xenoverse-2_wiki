<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Potential Unleashed: Advancement Test Guide">

Unlocking the **dragon ball xenoverse 2 potential unleashed** skill is a rite of passage for every serious Time Patroller. Unlike skills obtained through Parallel Quest drops, PU is a guaranteed reward for mastering the game's core combat mechanics. You must prove your worth at the Patroller Academy by conquering the Advancement Tests.

To trigger the final quest for Potential Unleashed, you must earn a **Z-Rank** on the first five tests. A Z-Rank is determined by your total score, which is influenced by clear time, health remaining, and the variety of techniques used during the fight.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Max Training Rank">
    Complete all available instructor lessons and story chapters to unlock the five basic Advancement Tests (Easy, Intermediate, Advanced, Kai, and God).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Z-Rank Requirements">
    To ensure a Z-Rank, aim for a 20-hit combo, finish with an Ultimate Attack, maintain over 80% health, and land at least three successful grabs or Just Guards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the Super Class Test">
    Once all previous tests show a Z-Rank, the "Super Class" Advancement Test will appear. This is a gauntlet against multiple high-tier mentors. Defeat them to receive the skill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Z-Rank Difficulty">
If you clear a test but receive an S-Rank or lower, you will not unlock the Super Class test. Replay the mission and focus on using "Just Guards" and "Stamina Breaks" to inflate your technical score.
</Callout>
