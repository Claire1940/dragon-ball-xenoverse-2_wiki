<Section icon={Target} title="Advancement Tests and Rank Progression">

You cannot simply walk up to every instructor and begin training. Your eligibility is determined by your rank at the Patroller Academy. As you complete missions and gain experience, you must return to the academy to take Advancement Tests. These tests serve as gatekeepers for higher-tier **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 instructors**.

| Rank | Test Name | Unlockable Mentors (Examples) | Reward |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Easy** | Test 1 | Yamcha, Tien, Krillin | New Skill Set |
| **Intermediate** | Test 2 | Gohan & Videl, Captain Ginyu | Increased Level Cap |
| **Advanced** | Test 3 | Android 18, Cell, Lord Slug | New Skill Set |
| **Senior** | Test 4 | Beerus, Majin Buu | New Skill Set |
| **Super** | Test 5 | Goku, Pan, Whis | **Potential Unleashed** |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Earn Total Training XP">
    Complete Parallel Quests and Story missions to fill your training meter located in the Play Data menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit the Academy Robot">
    Once the meter is full, speak to the robot in front of the Orange Star High School to initiate the next test.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Achieve Z-Rank">
    To unlock the final "Super" class test, you must achieve a Z-rank on all five previous Advancement Tests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Instructor Gauntlet">
    The final test requires you to defeat a series of mentors in a row. Success grants you the Potential Unleashed Awoken skill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Z-Rank Requirement">
Achieving a Z-rank requires more than just speed. You should focus on finishing with high health, using at least three grabs, and landing a 20+ hit combo during the test.
