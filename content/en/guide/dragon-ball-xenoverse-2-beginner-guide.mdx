Understanding the controls is the first step toward becoming a top-tier Time Patroller. The **Z-Dash** is a critical maneuver that allows you to dodge incoming projectiles and close the gap instantly. By double-tapping the dash button while holding a direction, you can circle around enemies, making it much harder for the CPU or human players to track your movements. Additionally, guard breaking is essential when facing defensive opponents. A standard guard break is performed by pushing the left stick upward while pressing the light attack button. If successful, this leaves the opponent in a "stamina broken" state, preventing them from vanishing out of your next combo.

| Control Type | PlayStation Input | Xbox Input | Effect in Combat |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Z-Dash** | L2 + X + Left Stick | LT + A + Left Stick | High-speed teleportation around target |
| **Guard Break** | Left Stick (Up) + Square | Left Stick (Up) + X | Breaks guard, drains enemy stamina |
| **Vanish** | X (During hit) | A (During hit) | Teleport behind attacker (Costs 2 Stamina bars) |
| **Descend** | L3 (Press) | LS (Press) | Quickly move toward the ground |
| **Ki Blast** | Circle | B | Fire a basic projectile to interrupt enemies |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Combat Tip">
Practice the timing of your vanishes. If you vanish too early in an opponent's combo, they can easily turn around and hit you again. Wait for a heavy hit or a predictable animation to make your escape count.

<Section icon={Target} title="Character Progression and Rapid Leveling">

Progression in **dragon ball xenoverse 2** is tied to your character's level and attribute points. While the early game allows for steady growth through the story, the late-game grind requires more specific strategies. One of the most important NPCs you will encounter is **Tosok**, a bearded character located behind the Skill Shop area. Tosok can increase your level instantly in exchange for currency. For levels 1 through 80, he accepts Zeni, which is easily earned through Parallel Quests (PQs). However, once you surpass level 80, he requires TP Medals, which are much harder to accumulate.

To maximize your experience gains without spending currency, you should seek out specific Super Souls. The Super Soul titled **"My hobbies are reading and sports"** is a game-changer for beginners. This item triples the score you receive at the end of a mission, effectively tripling your XP gain. While it negates any Zeni earned, the trade-off is well worth it for players looking to reach the level cap quickly. This Super Soul can often be obtained through Raids or by customizing mentor versions of Goku.

| Level Range | Required Currency | Best Farming Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1 - 50** | Zeni (Low cost) | Story Mode and Early PQs |
| **51 - 80** | Zeni (Moderate cost) | Parallel Quest 4: Prepare for the Attack |
| **81 - 120** | TP Medals | Expert Mission 16: Realm of the Gods (Goku) |
| **121 - 199** | High TP Medals | Expert Mission 17: Realm of the Gods (Vegeta) |

<Callout type="success" title="Leveling Strategy">
Always prioritize your Stamina attribute. Having at least 6 to 7 bars of stamina is vital for survival in online matches and difficult Expert Missions. Without stamina, you cannot use Vanish or Evasive skills, leaving you defenseless.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Equipment, QQ Bangs, and Mixing Capsules">

In the early days of the game, your stats were tied directly to the clothes you wore. This forced players to choose between looking good and having optimal stats. The **QQ Bang** system solves this problem. A QQ Bang is an equippable item that overrides all clothing stats, allowing you to wear any costume without penalty. For a beginner, understanding how to craft a high-level QQ Bang is essential for competitive play.

To create the best QQ Bangs (Level 6), you must travel to Capsule Corp and speak with Bulma at the mixing station. You will need two pieces of high-level clothing (usually Level 5) and a **Super Mix Capsule Z**. These capsules are primarily obtained by completing **Expert Missions** or Parallel Quest Tours online. The resulting QQ Bang can provide massive +5 boosts to multiple attributes while minimizing negative penalties.

| Ingredient 1 | Ingredient 2 | Mixing Item | Resulting Quality |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Level 5 Clothing | Level 5 Clothing | Super Mix Capsule Z | **Level 6 (Best)** |
| Level 4 Clothing | Level 4 Clothing | Super Mix Capsule | Level 4 or 5 |
| Level 2 Clothing | Level 2 Clothing | Mixing Capsule | Level 2 or 3 |
| Any Clothing | Any Clothing | Ultimate Mix Capsule | Random 3-5 |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mixing Warning">
Mixing is a game of chance. Even with the best ingredients, you might not get the perfect stat distribution on your first try. Always keep a backup of your favorite clothing items before mixing them away.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Online Play and Dragon Ball Farming">

While **dragon ball xenoverse 2** offers a robust offline experience, the online component is where the community thrives. However, new players should be aware of the requirements. Online play on consoles typically requires a subscription service like PlayStation Plus or Xbox Game Pass Core. If you do not have these, the game will automatically boot you into an "Offline Lobby." Note that the **Lite Version** of the game is essentially a demo; it restricts access to certain races (like Saiyans) and limits your level progression.

Farming the Dragon Balls is another core activity. These items allow you to reset your attribute points, unlock new characters (like Hit or Eis Shenron), or gain massive amounts of TP Medals. The most efficient way to farm them is by fighting **Time Patroller NPCs** that appear in specific Parallel Quests.

| Parallel Quest | Difficulty | Farming Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PQ 04**: Prepare for Attack | Very Easy | Defeat the three Time Patrollers at the start. |
| **PQ 23**: Explosion of Namek | Easy | Defeat Frieza quickly, then fight the Time Patrollers. |
| **PQ 79**: Great Ape Festival | Medium | Clear the first area and defeat the Time Patrollers. |
