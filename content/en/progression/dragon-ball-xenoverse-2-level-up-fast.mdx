<Section icon={Target} title="Parallel Quest 23: The Dragon Ball Shortcut">

The fastest way to **dragon ball xenoverse 2 level up fast** during the mid-game is not through direct XP farming, but through gathering Dragon Balls. Parallel Quest 23, "The Explosion of Namek," is the gold standard for this method. This mission allows you to defeat Frieza quickly and then challenge three Time Patrollers who have a high chance of dropping Dragon Balls as "Key Items."

Summoning Shenron allows you to choose the wish **"I want to grow!"** (1 level) or **"I want to grow more!"** (3 levels). This is particularly effective after level 80, where the experience requirements per level increase exponentially.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip High-Burst Skills">
    Use skills like **Bending Kamehameha** or **Super God Fist** to defeat Frieza in under 3 minutes. This triggers the Ultimate Finish.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Challenge the Time Patrollers">
    After Frieza falls, three Time Patrollers will appear near the landing craft. Defeat them one by one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check for Key Items">
    If you see "Key Item" appear on the screen after a Patroller is defeated, you have successfully obtained a Dragon Ball.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete and Repeat">
    Finish the mission to save your rewards. Repeat until you have all seven balls, then head to the Dragon Ball Pedestal in Conton City.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Ultimate Finish Requirement">
If you do not defeat Frieza within the 3-minute time limit, the Time Patrollers will not spawn. If you are struggling with damage, ensure your attribute points are focused on one primary damage type (Strike or Ki Blast).
</Callout>
