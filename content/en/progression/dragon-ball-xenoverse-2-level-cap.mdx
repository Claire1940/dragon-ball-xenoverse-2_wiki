<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking the Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 Level Cap">

Breaking through the various level barriers in the game is not automatic. New players will find themselves hitting artificial ceilings at levels 80, 85, 90, 95, and 99. To bypass these limits, you must complete specific in-game activities, primarily involving Grand Elder Guru and the Angel instructor, Whis. 

The progression route is divided into two major phases: the Guru Phase (levels 80–99) and the Whis Phase (levels 99–199). Guru handles the early cap breaks and expands your individual attribute limit from 100 to 125. Whis takes over once you reach the classic level 99 limit, demanding specific food items or high friendship levels to grant you access to the god-tier levels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Guru's House Defense Quests">
    Travel to the Namekian Time Rift and complete Grand Elder Guru's defense missions. Once you defend the Dragon Balls multiple times, Guru will grant you an audience. He will unlock your status, allowing you to allocate up to 125 points in a single attribute.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Break the Level 80 to 99 Barriers">
    Return to Guru every time you hit a level cap. He will manually raise your cap in increments: first to 85, then 90, 95, and finally 99. You must do this on your first character; subsequent characters will have these early caps unlocked automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maximize Friendship with Whis">
    Once you hit level 99, set Whis as your mentor and bring him along on Parallel Quests to maximize your friendship. Alternatively, you can buy a Parfait from the food shop and present it to him. Talk to him in the Patroller Academy area to unlock the path to level 120.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect TP Medals for the Final Cap Breaks">
    To progress from level 120 to 140, and ultimately to the level 199 cap, you must speak with Whis while possessing a substantial amount of TP Medals. He will perform divine training rituals that permanently raise your level cap to the current maximum.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Guru's House Priority">
Do not ignore Guru's House notifications. If you fail to defend Guru's House when prompted, your progress toward unlocking the attribute cap will temporarily halt. Complete these missions immediately whenever they appear on your screen.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Speed Leveling Routes and Shenron Wishes">

Grinding experience points naturally to reach level 199 is an incredibly daunting task. The experience required per level scales exponentially past level 99. To save dozens of hours of repetitive grinding, players should utilize two primary shortcut methods: the level-up NPC Tosok, and summoning Shenron using the seven Dragon Balls.

Tosok is a Namekian located behind the accessory shop in Conton City. He will increase your level instantly in exchange for Zeni (for lower levels) or TP Medals (for higher levels). For players who prefer not to spend TP Medals, farming Dragon Balls to make leveling wishes is the most efficient alternative.
