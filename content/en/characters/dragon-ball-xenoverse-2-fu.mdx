<Section icon={BookOpen} title="Origins and Character Profile">

**Dragon ball xenoverse 2 Fu** is one of the most enigmatic characters in the franchise. Originally conceived for the defunct *Dragon Ball Online*, he made his full debut in *Xenoverse 2* as a brilliant, if somewhat reckless, scientist. Unlike his parents, Fu isn't inherently evil; he is driven by an insatiable curiosity to see how history changes when he introduces new variables.

| Attribute | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Race** | Demon-Saiyan Hybrid (Artificial) |
| **Parents** | Mira and Towa |
| **First Appearance** | Extra Pack 2 (Xenoverse 2) |
| **Voice Actor (EN)** | Ricco Fajardo |
| **Voice Actor (JP)** | Kappei Yamaguchi |
| **Key Equipment** | Time Cutter (Short Sword) |

Fu's physiology is a complex blend of DNA from the universe's strongest fighters, including **Goku**, **Vegeta**, **Frieza**, and **Mechikabura**. This genetic makeup grants him immense power, though he often prefers observing battles over participating in them. In the 2026 meta, he remains a fan favorite due to his unique personality and reality-warping abilities.

<Callout type="tip" title="Lore Note">

### Super and Ultimate Attacks

Fu's toolkit is built for utility. His **Confusion Blade** is particularly notorious in PvP for its delayed tracking, forcing opponents to stay on the move.

| Skill Type | Name | Effect Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Super Attack** | Confusion Blade | Fires dark fireballs that home in after a delay. |
| **Super Attack** | Sneaky Strike | Turns invisible and delivers a diving blade strike. |
| **Super Attack** | Energy Minefield | Scatters mines that electrocute on contact. |
| **Ultimate Attack** | Remote Serious Bomb | Plants a bomb that seals the target's transformation. |
| **Ultimate Attack** | Dual Remote Serious Bomb | A team-based version of his signature seal. |
| **Evasive** | Android Kick | A dark energy-enhanced flying kick to escape pressure. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Warning">
The **Remote Serious Bomb** is his most dangerous tool. If it hits, it forces the opponent out of their Super Saiyan or Potential Unleashed forms and prevents them from re-transforming for a limited time.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="The Infinite History Saga Walkthrough">

Fu is the catalyst for the **Infinite History Saga**, a branching storyline where players can choose to either assist Fu in his experiments or follow the instructions of the Time Patrol. Your choices directly affect the ending and the rewards you receive.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The First Time Rift">
    Meet Fu in the Time Nest. He asks you to help him gather **Rift Energy**. The first mission involves altering the training of Goku and Vegeta to induce a Super Saiyan 4 transformation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Branching Paths">
    In each subsequent rift (Cell Games, Majin Buu Saga), you can choose to follow Fu's plan or fix the history. Assisting Fu provides more energy but upsets the Elder Kai.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Final Confrontation">
    Depending on your choices, you will either fight alongside Fu against Dabura or face Fu himself as he tests your strength in a friendly (but intense) duel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Ending Variations

There are three primary endings to Fu's storyline in the Infinite History expansion.

| Ending | Requirement | Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ending 1** | Follow Fu's instructions | Fu creates an illusion to fight you and escapes with the energy. |
| **Ending 2** | Follow Elder Kai (Goku not Master) | You fight Goku to protect Fu while he analyzes your data. |
| **Ending 3** | Follow Elder Kai (Goku as Master) | You fight Ultra Instinct Goku; Fu gives you energy to win. |

<Callout type="success" title="Completionist Tip">
To achieve 100% completion in the Infinite History Saga, you must play through every mission with every possible mentor, as mentors provide unique dialogue and interactions with Fu.
</Callout>
