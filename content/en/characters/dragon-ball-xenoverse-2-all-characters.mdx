<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide: Summoning Shenron's Exclusive Fighters">

Unlocking the four secret characters—Hit, Eis Shenron, Nuova Shenron, and Omega Shenron—requires gathering the seven Dragon Balls and summoning Shenron at the Dragon Ball Pedestal in Conton City. Follow this optimized route to complete your roster quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build a Fast Farming Loadout">
    Equip your custom character with high-damage, fast-tracking Ki Blast supers (such as Bending Kamehameha or Bomber DX) and a rapid charge skill like Burst Charge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Parallel Quest 23">
    Select "PQ 23 — The Explosion of Namek." Defeat Frieza immediately. If you clear this opening fight in under three minutes, a portal will open, and three Time Patrollers will spawn in training.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Time Patrollers">
    Challenge and defeat all three Time Patrollers. Focus on them one at a time. Watch the bottom-right of your screen for a "Key Item" notification after each defeat—this indicates a Dragon Ball drop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Summon Shenron and Wish">
    Take the seven Dragon Balls to the pedestal in the center of Conton City. Summon Shenron and select the wish **"I Want More Usable Characters!"** to unlock the next secret fighter in the fixed sequence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Wish Order">
You must repeat this farming process and summon Shenron four separate times to unlock all four secret characters. The unlock order is strictly fixed: Hit, Eis Shenron, Nuova Shenron, and Omega Shenron.
</Callout>

</Section>
