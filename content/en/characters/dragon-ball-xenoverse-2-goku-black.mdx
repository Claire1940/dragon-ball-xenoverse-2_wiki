<Section icon={Map} title="Future Trunks Saga Story Mode Walkthrough">

The Future Trunks Saga is a pivotal chapter in the main campaign of Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2. Players must assist Future Trunks, Goku, and Vegeta in combatting the combined threat of Goku Black and Zamasu. This saga features multiple phases where the timeline has been altered, resulting in tougher enemies and unexpected allies.

To successfully navigate this saga, players must be prepared for high-difficulty fights where enemies frequently use health regeneration, super armor, and coordinated double-team attacks.

<Callout type="warning" title="Boss Battle Warning">
During the final phases of this saga, Zamasu is completely immortal and cannot have his health reduced to zero. Focus your attacks entirely on Goku Black to progress the fight and trigger the next cutscene.
</Callout>
