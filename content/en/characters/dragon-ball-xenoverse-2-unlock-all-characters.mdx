<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unlocking Secret and Event Characters">

Some characters in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 are not found in the story or PQs. These "Secret" characters require participation in specific online events or the use of the TP Medal Shop.

**Chronoa (Supreme Kai of Time)** is perhaps the rarest character to unlock. She is tied exclusively to the **Conton City Tournament**. This event is an online-only world tournament that occurs roughly 2-3 times per year. To unlock her, you must complete the tournament twice when it is active. Because there is no fixed schedule, players must check the News Board in Conton City regularly.

**Raid Bosses and Crystal Raids**
You can also unlock specific "Raid Boss" versions of characters like Demigra, Fused Zamasu (Half-Corrupted), and Mira (Final Form) for use in Training Mode and Crystal Raids.

| Character | Unlock Method | Cost / Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chronoa** | Conton City Tournament | Beat the event twice |
| **Demigra** | TP Medal Shop Gift | 500 TP Medals |
| **Fused Zamasu** | TP Medal Shop Gift | 500 TP Medals |
| **Mira (Final Form)** | TP Medal Shop Gift | 500 TP Medals |

<Callout type="success" title="Success">
Once you have purchased the gifts for the Raid Bosses from the TP Medal Shop, take them to **Fu** in the Time Rift area to finalize the unlock. Note that these characters are restricted to Crystal Raids and Training Mode.
</Callout>
