### The Super Soul: "I don't want them to get hurt!"

This Super Soul is the backbone of Android 21's sustainability. It triggers health recovery and provides a boost to all stats when certain conditions are met, making her a "tanky" character despite her slender frame.

*   **Trigger**: Activates when health drops below 50%.
*   **Effect**: Small auto-health recovery and a Medium boost to all attacks.
*   **Utility**: Excellent for surviving Ultimate attacks from opponents in PvP.

### Recommended Combo Strings

1.  **The Slicing Loop**: Light Attack (x5) > Savory Slicer > Dash > Light Attack (x5). This loop is great for building Ki while keeping the opponent in a hit-stun state.
2.  **The Guard Breaker**: Heavy Attack (x2) > Photon Swipe. If the opponent blocks the heavy attacks, the Photon Swipe often catches them off guard and breaks their stamina.
3.  **Ultimate Finisher**: Light Attack (x7) > Heavy Attack (x1) > Excellent Full Course. This is her most reliable way to land her ultimate without needing a stamina break.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Android 21 relies heavily on her Evasive, "Total Evasion." While powerful, it consumes 3 bars of stamina. Never let your stamina drop below 3 bars unless you are certain you can finish the fight.
</Callout>
