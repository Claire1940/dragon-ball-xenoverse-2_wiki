<Section icon={Target} title="Conquering Broly Parallel Quests and Expert Missions">

Broly is not just a powerful ally; he is also one of the most formidable boss encounters in the game. Players must defeat him in various high-difficulty missions to farm rare skills, Super Souls, and Dragon Balls. The two most notable encounters are Parallel Quest 83 and Expert Mission 18.

Parallel Quest 83, titled "Dangerous Duo! Warriors Never Rest," requires players to recover three Dragon Balls while surviving Broly's relentless attacks. To secure the Ultimate Finish, you must defeat Broly while keeping Gohan alive, then recover all seven Dragon Balls.

Expert Mission 18 presents a much steeper challenge, pitting a team of players or AI partners against a super-armored Raid Boss Broly. This boss utilizes dangerous raid hazards such as Peeler Storm and Gigantic Ki Blasts, which can wipe out an unprepared team in seconds.
