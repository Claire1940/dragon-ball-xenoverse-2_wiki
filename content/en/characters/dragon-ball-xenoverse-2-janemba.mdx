<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Combo Strings & Damage Tactics">

Mastering Janemba requires more than just button mashing. You need to utilize his teleportation frames to bypass enemy defenses. The most effective way to secure a win is by executing a "Stamina Break" followed by a high-damage sequence.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Setup">
    Initiate with a Light combo chain (5-6 hits) until the opponent is staggered. If they attempt to vanish, be ready to back-hit or use a teleport dash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Stamina Break">
    Perform a **Vanish Stamina Break** (forward + light attack) during the stagger animation. This is critical to ensure the enemy cannot use an Evasive skill during your Ultimate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Grounding">
    Immediately follow the stamina break with **Sledgehammer**. This will slam the enemy into the floor, leaving them vulnerable for several seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Finisher">
    While the enemy is grounded, activate **Dimension Ray**. Because the enemy is in a "hard knockdown" state, the shards from Dimension Ray will almost always connect for full damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Combo Sequence | Total Damage | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Light Combo -> Sledgehammer -> Rakshasa's Claw | 8,624 | Medium |
| Light Stamina Break -> Sledgehammer -> Full Power Energy Wave | 9,722 | High |
| Rushing Stamina Break -> Heavy Knockdown -> Dimension Ray | 8,500+ | Easy |
