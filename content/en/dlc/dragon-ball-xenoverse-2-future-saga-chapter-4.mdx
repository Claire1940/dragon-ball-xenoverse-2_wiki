<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Strategy: Handling the New Mechanics">

The "Future Saga" update introduces environmental hazards that can significantly alter the flow of a fight. In **dragon ball xenoverse 2 future saga chapter 4**, you will encounter "Timeline Fractures." These are localized zones that can either drain your Ki or boost your movement speed. Understanding how to manipulate these zones is the difference between victory and defeat in high-level play.

**Strategic Considerations:**
- **Ki Drains**: If you find yourself in a purple-tinted zone, your Ki will rapidly deplete. Avoid using Ultimates while standing in these areas.
- **Speed Rifts**: Blue-tinted zones provide a 20% boost to movement and dash speed. Use these to close the distance on "Zoners" who rely on long-range Ki blasts.
- **Fu's Traps**: Fu will occasionally place "Research Cubes" on the battlefield. Destroying these prevents him from healing or gaining a temporary defense buff.
