<Section icon={Target} title="How to Master Ultra Super Villain Mechanics">

Mastering these new characters requires a shift in how you view the health bar. In **dragon ball xenoverse 2 dlc**, the Ultra Super Villain Super Soul is a double-edged sword. You gain a massive boost to all attributes, but your health will steadily tick down.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Aggression">
    At the start of the match, your Super Soul is most active. Use Goku's high-speed multi-hits or Chronoa's projectiles to pressure the opponent immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stamina Management">
    Because you are losing health, you cannot afford to have your stamina broken. Use **Celestial Wave** or **Chaos Wall** to reset the neutral game if you get caught.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Low Health Buffs">
    Once your health drops below 25%, skills like **Dragon Spiral** gain enhanced properties. This is your "comeback" window where your damage output is at its peak.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Limit Burst Timing">
    Goku’s **Revive Gauge Auto Recovery** is vital in 2v2 or 3v3 matches. Use it if a teammate falls to quickly bring them back into the fight while you distract the enemies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Super Soul Logic">
The health drain is not permanent. Once the Super Soul "burns out," the drain stops, but so does the massive power boost. The character returns to a "standard" power level for the remainder of the fight.
</Callout>

</Section>
