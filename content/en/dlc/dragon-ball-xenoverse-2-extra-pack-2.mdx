<Section icon={Sparkles} title="New Characters & Playstyle Breakdown">

The roster additions in this expansion bring some of the most iconic powerhouses from the Tournament of Power directly into your hands. Each character features unique combat mechanics, distinct combo routes, and highly specialized super attacks that can turn the tide of any battle. 

Understanding how these characters function is essential for both PvE content and competitive PvP matches. Goku in his Ultra Instinct form relies heavily on defensive auto-dodges, while Jiren acts as an unstoppable strike-based tank. Fu introduces highly technical sword-based setups, and Android 17 (Ranger) provides excellent defensive utility and zone control.

### Goku (Ultra Instinct)
Goku in his Ultra Instinct form is the crown jewel of this pack. His unique passive ability allows him to automatically dodge basic physical attacks and basic ki blasts at the cost of Stamina. This makes him incredibly difficult to hit, but players must manage their Stamina carefully, as breaking his guard leaves him highly vulnerable. His Ultimate Attack, Godly Display, unleashes a rapid flurry of strikes followed by a massive, tracking energy sphere that deals devastating damage.

### Jiren
Jiren is a pure physical powerhouse designed to overwhelm opponents with raw strength. His signature move, Power Rush, is one of the most damaging Strike Ultimates in the game, though it requires a full 10 bars of Ki to execute. To support this massive requirement, Jiren can use Meditation, a buff skill that temporarily boosts max Ki and accelerates Ki regeneration.

### Fu
Fu is a highly technical fighter who excels at keeping opponents guessing. Utilizing his ninja sword and scientific gadgets, Fu can teleport across the battlefield and set energy traps. His Sneaky Strike allows him to disappear mid-combo and strike from an unexpected angle, making him a favorite for competitive players who prefer a deceptive playstyle.

### Android 17 (Ranger)
This version of Android 17, wearing his park ranger outfit from Dragon Ball Super, focuses on barrier-based defense and fast-paced counter-attacks. He can generate energy shields that absorb incoming damage and convert it into ki, allowing him to sustain long combos and pressure opponents continuously.

<Callout type="info" title="Stamina Management Warning">
While Ultra Instinct Goku auto-dodges basic attacks, each dodge drains a portion of your Stamina bar. Avoid passive play, as skilled opponents will bait your dodges to force a stamina break.
</Callout>
