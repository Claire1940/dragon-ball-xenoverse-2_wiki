<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock the Super Saiyan God Transformation">

Unlocking the **dragon ball xenoverse 2 super saiyan god** form is a multi-step process that requires a combination of Dragon Ball farming, social interaction with mentors, and specific story progression. Unlike the standard Super Saiyan 1-3 forms which are unlocked through simple training with Vegeta, the God form requires divine intervention via Shenron.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect the Seven Dragon Balls">
    The fastest way to gather Dragon Balls in 2026 remains **PQ 23: The Explosion of Namek**. Defeat Frieza in under three minutes to trigger a Time Patroller encounter. Defeating these three NPCs gives you a high chance of receiving a "Key Item" drop. Repeat this until you have all seven.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Make the Divine Wish">
    Head to the Dragon Ball Pedestal in Conton City. Summon Shenron and select the wish: **"I want to become a Super Saiyan God!"** Note that this wish only appears if you are playing as a Saiyan character and have progressed sufficiently in the main story.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maximize Mentor Friendships">
    Shenron will tell you that you need the power of five other Saiyans. You must maximize your friendship levels with the following instructors: Goku, Vegeta, Gohan & Videl, Gotenks, and Pan.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consult with Lord Beerus">
    Once all five friendships are at 100%, locate Beerus at the Patroller Academy or his usual spot near the Orange Star High School area. Speak with him, and he will grant you the Awoken Skill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Friendship Requirements">
Maximizing friendship can be tedious. To speed this up, equip your mentor's skills, bring two copies of that mentor as AI partners in Parallel Quests, and talk to them after every mission.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering the Timed Hit Mechanic">

The defining feature of **dragon ball xenoverse 2 super saiyan god** is the "Flash" or "Timed Hit" mechanic. When you perform certain combo strings, a red flash will appear on the final hit. If you time your button press perfectly with this flash, your character will perform a more powerful version of the finisher.

1.  **Identify the Finisher**: Most Saiyan light-light-light-heavy-heavy strings end in a kick or heavy punch that triggers the flash.
2.  **Visual Cues**: Watch for the red circular aura that expands from your fist or foot.
3.  **The Reward**: A successful timed hit deals roughly 15-20% more damage than a standard hit and often provides better knockback for following up with a Super.
4.  **Practice**: Head to the Training Mode and set the CPU to "Stand". Practice your basic strings until the red flash becomes muscle memory.

<Callout type="tip" title="Advanced Tactic">
Because SSG increases movement speed and Ki recovery, you can use a timed hit to knock an opponent away, then immediately use a **Burst Dash** or **Vanishing Ball** to continue the pressure without ever running out of resources.
</Callout>
